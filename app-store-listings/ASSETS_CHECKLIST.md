# Store Assets Checklist (Play Store + App Store)

## Required Visual Assets

### Shared
- [ ] App icon master (1024x1024 PNG)
- [ ] Brand color/background variants
- [ ] Light and dark mode mockups (if applicable)

### Google Play
- [ ] App icon (512x512 PNG)
- [ ] Feature graphic (1024x500 PNG)
- [ ] Phone screenshots (min 2, recommended 6-8)
- [ ] 7-inch tablet screenshots (optional but recommended)
- [ ] 10-inch tablet screenshots (optional but recommended)

### Apple App Store
- [ ] App icon (1024x1024 PNG, no alpha)
- [ ] iPhone screenshots (6.7" and/or 6.5")
- [ ] iPad screenshots (if iPad supported)
- [ ] App preview video (optional)

## Suggested Screenshot Sequence
1. Home marketplace discovery
2. Product details + seller view
3. Services discovery
4. Wholesale section
5. Seller dashboard/tools
6. Fast contact/support flow

## Screenshot Copy (short overlays)
- "Buy and sell in one place"
- "Discover trusted sellers"
- "Explore services and wholesale"
- "Grow your business with Pambo"

## Compliance
- [ ] Privacy policy page live and reachable
- [ ] Terms page live and reachable
- [ ] Support contact page live
- [ ] No misleading claims in screenshots
